Ingredients

  • Crushed tomatoes (400 g)
  • Oregano
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Minced meat (500 g)
  • Mozarella

Steps

  1. Start oven for 200 degrees
  2. Chop onion and garlic
  3. Fry the Minced meat until it starts to get color
  4. Add the chopped onion and garlic. It should fry until golden
  5. Pour crushed tomatoes into the pan
  6. Pour content to heatproof container, top with Mozzarellallalla
  7. Put container in the oven, cook for 25 minutes
