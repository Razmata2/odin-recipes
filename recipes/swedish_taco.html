Ingredients

  • Ground beef (500 g)
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Tomato (diced, 1 medium)
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Chili powder
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Small tortillas
  • Cheddar cheese (grated)
  • Lettuce (shredded)
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Chop onion and garlic
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat
  3. Fry the minced meat until it starts to brown
  4. Add the chopped onion and garlic, cook until golden
  5. Season with oregano, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper
  6. Stir in diced tomato and let simmer for a few minutes
  7. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave
  8. Fill each tortilla with the meat mixture
  9. Top with shredded lettuce, cheese, salsa, and sour cream
  10. Serve and enjoy!
